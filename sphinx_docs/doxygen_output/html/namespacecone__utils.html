Eigen::MatrixXd cone_utils::calc_cone_bearing_from_car ( const std::vector< gtsam::Point2 > &  cone_obs)

Calculates the bearing angle from the car to each cone in radians.

Parameters
cone_obsVector of cone positions relative to the car.
Returns
Eigen::MatrixXd Vector of bearings for each cone.

Intuition: Perceptions gives us cones in DV axes: y forwards and x right

Directly in front of us, the bearing is 0

  • The bearing is the angle from the axis directly in front of us
  • counter clockwise is positive bearing
  • clockwise is negative bearing

Given the DV axes, to calculate the bearing is atan2(-x, y)

  • If the bearing was to the left (counter clockwise from 0)
  • The bearing would be a positive angle
